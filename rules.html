Rules

______________________________

Choose a category below to view the rules.
Got a question about our rules? Join our Discord and open a ticket!


Minecraft

  Client Mods:

  Allowed
  -Client mods that do not give you an unfair advantage: Minimaps, Optifine, Gamma mods, FPS Boosters, etc
  -Use at your own discretion. Any issues that arise from client issues cannot be helped by a staff member

  Not Allowed
  -All other client mods (such as fly, jump, speed, x-ray, auto clickers, etc.) are considered against the rules
  -If you're caught using illegal mods, you will either be warned or banned, depending on the situation


  Griefing/stealing:

  Allowed
  -Heavily discouraged, however, you can only grief unclaimed bases with an inactive owner for minimum of 30 days. Ask a mod for help or open a help ticket

  Not allowed
  -Griefing or stealing from a claimed base you are trusted in is not allowed.Grief/stolen items from a trusted individual will not have staff interference
  -No claim blocking- Claiming outside a players base without permission. All builds must be 150 blocks away from the next claim


  Advertisement:

  Allowed
  -You are allowed to record and stream content from BlankMC
  -Advertising a town or build on BlankMC

  Not allowed
  -It is forbidden to advertise any servers, discords, or mentioning server IPs anywhere except for #self-promo on the discord


  Abuse of bugs:

  Allowed
  -Only allowed to replicate a bug or glitch for the purpose of reporting to staff

  Not allowed
  -Use of bugs or glitches aren't allowed unless for purposes noted above


  Harassment and any chat related offenses:

  -Any chat or nickname that have profanity, sexual innuendos, mimicking other players or staff are against the rules
  -Mild jokes are acceptable unless someone asks to stop or is offended
  -Spamming Chat
  -Encouraging violence towards anyone
  -Sharing other players private information


  Build Restrictions:

  -Iron farms have a maximum of 20 villagers in a 16 chunk radius
  -Redstone builds will have a max of 40 per chunk: Hoppers, observers, droppers, etc.
  -Floating islands may be built above your land only
  -Flying machines must 100% contained
  -No flying machine TNT dupers
  -Building hate symbols
  -0 tick farms


  Miscellaneous offenses:

  -Using loopholes to violate rules
  -Falsifying reports
  -Arguing with final decisions made by staff
  -Alternate Accounts


  General Reminder:

  -Be nice to other players. Treat others the way you want to be treated
  -Use common sense


Discord

  -Avoid using derogatory language this discord is meant to be community friendly any kind of offensive language, insults, name calling, and excessive swearing is prohibited
  -Do not be annoying and spam
  -Do not post links to other servers, their IPs, other discords that aren't related to BlankMC. We have a self promotion channel dedicated for that
  -Do not be obnoxious in our voice chats, we also do not tolerate harassment of any kind and ear rape
  -The BlankMC Discord server abides by all of the Terms of Service set by discord. Ensure that the ToS is being followed when on our server
  -We do not allow discussion of illegal activities and NSFW content
  -We have a zero tolerance policy against impersonation of staff and their abilities
